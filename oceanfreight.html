Ocean Freight Services

Ocean Freight remains the backbone of international trade, handling over 80% of global shipments. At Watraco Logistics, we specialize in providing dependable and cost-effective sea transportation solutions that connect businesses and customers across continents.

We collaborate with trusted global carriers and port partners to guarantee timely deliveries, transparent communication, and flexible scheduling options

Every container is tracked with cutting-edge technology, allowing our clients to monitor their cargo in real time through our online tracking system. From the moment your goods leave the port of origin to their safe arrival at the destination, we keep you informed every step of the way.

Our experienced logistics team handles all aspects of the shipping process — documentation, customs clearance, and port operations — so you can focus on your business. We understand that reliability and efficiency are crucial, especially when dealing with time-sensitive or high-value shipments.

At Watraco, we don’t just move containers; we build trust. Our commitment to quality service has made us a preferred partner for importers, exporters, and global brands. Whether your business needs weekly departures, temperature-controlled shipping, or heavy machinery transport, we have the expertise and network to make it happen seamlessly.

We believe that great logistics should be invisible — running quietly in the background while your business thrives. With Watraco Ocean Freight, your goods are in the safest hands across the world’s oceans.

Our ocean freight operations prioritize reliability and transparency. With strategic port connections and optimized route planning, we minimize transit times and reduce costs for clients.

Beyond standard shipping, Watraco provides tailored marine logistics solutions — including full container loads (FCL) and less-than-container loads (LCL). Our expert team manages customs, documentation, and port coordination, ensuring a smooth, worry-free process for importers and exporters across the globe.

Why Choose Ocean Freight

Our ocean freight services offer unmatched cost-efficiency, reliability, and capacity for large shipments. With global routes, secure tracking, and expert handling, we ensure your goods arrive safely and on schedule — every time, across every ocean.
